How to Play

As a whole, there are no winners or losers.

1. CREATE your activity

In the next screen, type out your idea for anything you want the group to do together. It can bea game, an adventure, something simple, something complicated...Anything you can imagine! The only rules are that it needs to be something the entire group is comfortable doing and feasible to do within the allotted time frame.

2. PICK an activity

The Taskmaster will select one of the activities at random for all to participate in. If needed, the original creator of the activity may give further explanation.

3. DO that activity

Particpate and have fun! Once the time is up, you'll move on to the next random actvity, and repeat until the game is done!
